9. MULTIPLE FOR EV MANUFACTURING 
a. EV Multiple shall be applied to Basic Investment Promotion Assistance 
(BIPA) for industries engaged in Electric Vehicle (EV) manufacturing. 
b. For verification of plant and machinery of EV manufacturing, the unit 
shall furnish a certificate issued by Chartered Engineer. 
c. The value of EV Multiple is fixed at 1.3 for EV manufacturing units. 
This means such industries will receive 30% additional financial 
assistance over standard BIPA incentives. 
10. 
MULTIPLE FOR HVA 
a. High Value-Add Manufacturing Multiple (HVA Multiple) shall be 
applied to Basic Investment Promotion Assistance (BIPA) for industries 
engaged in the production of high-value, high-tech, and innovation-
driven products. 
b. High Value-add manufacturing shall carry the same meaning as 
defined in chapter 9.10.1 of the IPP, 2025. 
c. The value of HVA Multiple is fixed at 1.3 for high-value-add 
manufacturing units. This means such industries will receive 30% 
additional financial assistance over standard BIPA incentives.
