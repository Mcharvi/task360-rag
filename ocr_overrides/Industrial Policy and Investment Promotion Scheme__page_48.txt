b. Green Industrialization Assistance:  
o Capital Subsidy for Waste Management Systems:  
 For 
setting 
up 
of 
Common

infrastructure, including Effluent Treatment Plants 
(ETP), Sewage Treatment Plants (STP), and pollution

Waste Management Systems, with a maximum limit of ₹5

 A capital subsidy of 50% on investment made for setting

annual instalments. 
c. Subsidy on Common Facility Centres:  
 Developers shall be eligible for capital assistance of 25% 
on the investment made for the establishment of 
Common Facility Centres (CFCs). 
  The SLEC shall define the CFCs based on the project 
report on case-to-case basis.
