The State has undertaken projects to enhance its transport infrastructure. Key developments include the
Chambal Expressway, Indore-Jhabua (NH-53), and various bypass roads around mgjor cities such as
Indore and Bhopal. Additionally, the state is enhancing connectivity with neighboring states through
projects like the Gwalior-Jhansi (NH 75), Mangawan (Rewa) - Uttar Pradesh border (NH27), and Seoni -
Maharashtra border (NH7).
Rail connectivity is also being strengthened with projects under Western Central Railways, such as the
electrification of the Itarsi-Manikpur section, new lines, doubling and tripling of key sections and various
other initiatives.
