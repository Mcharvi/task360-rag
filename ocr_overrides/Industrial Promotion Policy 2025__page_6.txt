The Madhya Pradesh Industrial Promotion Policy 2025 was developed through extensive analysis, 
benchmarking against leading States and adopting good practices. It follows an investor-centric approach, 
aligning with industry needs. Initiated in 2024, the Department of Industrial Policy and Investment Promotion 
hosted Regional Industrial Conclaves across Madhya Pradesh and conducted investor interactions at national 
and international levels. Industrialists were consulted to identify key challenges, which the policy aims to 
address.
