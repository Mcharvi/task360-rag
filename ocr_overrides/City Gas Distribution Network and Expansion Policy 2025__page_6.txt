Message

It is a matter of great pleasure that Department of Food, Civil Supplies and
Consumer Protection has issued the City Gas Distribution (CGD) Policy 2025 to
ensure access to domestic gas to households through pipeline network in Madhya
Pradesh. This policy facilitates not only the access of gas to every household in
the state, but also contributes a lot in development by expanding CNG gas stations
making clean fuel available for vehicles.This policy will expand clean energy in the state, thereby making a great
contribution of the state in environment protection. It is noteworthy that this
policy will not only attract investors and identify the state as India’s leading green
energy hub, but will also be beneficial for the health of the people of the state by
providing clean fuel to the people.

| am confident that this policy will lead to the construction of a large-scale
infrastructure in the state, along with providing employment opportunities to the
people of the state. On this occasion, | express my heartfelt wishes to the people
of the state.

(Govind Singh Rajput)
