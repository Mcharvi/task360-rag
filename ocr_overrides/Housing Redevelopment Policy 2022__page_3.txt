being in a condition that can be restored or repaired. Hence, declared dilapidated by
the concerned ULB.

3.11. Premium meansthe financial amount quoted by the PPP developer in response to

agency, where land ownership rights or lease ownership of land is not with public
authority. Such housing schemes can be considered under the policy, subject to the
existence of a Resident Welfare Association (RWA) in the said housing scheme
3.13. Public housing scheme means a housing scheme developed by any public agency
