12.5   PUBLIC CHARGING STATIONS 
The following steps will be undertaken to establish a public charging and battery-swapping 
station throughout Madhya Pradesh: 
(i) Charge Point Operators (CPOs), battery swapping operators (BSO) or a combination of 
both, will be invited to bid to set up charging stations along with battery-swapping 
facilities at each of the identified location. 
(ii) State Government shall facilitate land to service providers for setting up Charging/ 
Battery swapping facilities (as per a future plan that establishes a rational cap on such 
facilities).
