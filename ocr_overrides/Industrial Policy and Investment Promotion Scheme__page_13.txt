3.16. MPIDC 
“MP Industrial Development Corporation (MPIDC)” refers to MP Industrial 
Development Corporation (MPIDC), formed and incorporated under the 
Companies Act, 1956, which is under the Department of Industrial Policy 
and Investment Promotion, Government of MP.
