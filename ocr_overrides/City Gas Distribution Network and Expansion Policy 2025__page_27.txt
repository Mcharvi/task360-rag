12.3. If any unauthorized person/entity damages the gas pipeline/installations, then
based on the CGD entity’s written complaint the Police will immediately file the

safety.

the gas pipeline leakage/damage, etc will be given the status of Emergency
Vehicles by the Transport Department so that they can reach the spot without
any delay.

12.7. The CGD Entity shall take all precautionary measures for safety of the gas
pipelines along the roads during the operation period or during maintenance.

12.8 Due to the possibility of accident due to leakage from the gas pipeline, it will be
