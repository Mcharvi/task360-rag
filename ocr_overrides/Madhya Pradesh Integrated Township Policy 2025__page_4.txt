MESSAGE 
The Government of Madhya Pradesh recognizes the need for a comprehensive and 
strategic approach to urban development that ensures planned growth, economic 
sustainability, and improved quality of life for its citizens. In this context, the concept of 
Integrated Townships emerges as a transformative solution to address the growing demand 
for urban infrastructure and affordable housing while fostering sustainable development. 
Currently, urban land management in Madhya Pradesh is predominantly governed by 
the public sector, urban land management also requires direct people and private participation 
and this policy provides that. To overcome these challenges, the state government has 
formulated the Madhya Pradesh Integrated Township Policy, aiming to streamline the 
development process and encourage private sector participation in building well-planned, self-
sufficient townships. 
The objective of the Madhya Pradesh Integrated Township Policy is to promote 
inclusivity, sustainability, and economic viability and developing cities as urban growth hub 
through integration of residential and work center without compromising the public amenity 
areas. 
A key feature of this policy is Land Pooling, which allows private developers and 
landowners to collaborate in the development of urban infrastructure. This approach optimizes 
land utilization, minimizes upfront investment burdens, and enhances capital efficiency for 
employment generation. Furthermore, the policy facilitates the provision of external 
infrastructure such as roads, water supply, and power through government agencies, ensuring 
seamless integration with existing urban frameworks. 
The policy establishes an Empowered Committee for township approvals to ensure 
transparency and efficiency in project execution. Additionally, to promote environmental 
sustainability, incentives such as Green FAR, non-conventional energy provisions, and 
affordable housing benefits have been incorporated. 
Through this initiative, the Madhya Pradesh Government aims to achieve a balanced 
urban ecosystem where integrated townships contribute to economic growth, generate 
employment opportunities, and provide well-planned residential and workspaces. This policy 
is a step towards modern urban planning that aligns with global best practices while catering 
to the specific needs of Madhya Pradesh. 
It is our hope that the Madhya Pradesh Integrated Township Policy will serve as a 
guiding framework for developers, policymakers, and stakeholders in shaping the future of 
urban development in the state. With collective efforts and a shared vision, we look forward to 
fostering sustainable, vibrant, and livable urban spaces for the citizens of Madhya Pradesh.
