Message

We are delighted to introduce the Integrated Township
Policy for Madhya Pradesh, 2025, aimed at unlocking the potential
for sustainable urban development and real estate growth.
Considering its significant contribution to the state's GDP and its
high employment generation capacity, it was imperative to
formulate a policy that addresses the sector's challenges

As our cities grow, it is imperative to ensure that urban
expansion happens in a structured and inclusive manner, balancing
economic prosperity with social equity and environmental
sustainabilityThis policy serves as a strategic roadmap to fulfill the
housing demand, development of city/sub-city level social and
physical infrastructure. It seeks to streamline procedures to
enhance clarity, transparency, and accountability Through this
Initiative, the government aims to eliminate obstacles, encourage
greater investment, and create employment opportunities in the
state and promoting entrepreneurship for farmers and small
landholders to pool their land for planned development.

I urge all stakeholders to leverage the opportunities
provided by this policy and contribute to the creation of
world-class urban spaces that reflect the aspirations of our people.
Together, we will shape a brighter future for Madhya Pradesh.Let us all collaborate to build a robust ecosystem for the
holistic development of Madhya Pradesh. a
