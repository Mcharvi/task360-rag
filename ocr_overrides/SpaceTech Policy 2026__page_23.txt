8.2.5. Design Linked Incentive (DLI) for SoaceTech Applications
To support startups, MSMEs, and enterprises engaged in the design and development of new
products, systems, and applications in the SpaceTech sector, including satellite systems, payloads,
ground segment software, SSA/SDA tools like Al-driven collision alerts, debris removal prototypes, orbital
simulation software, and space robotics.
i. Incentive Structure
- Milestone-linked reimbursement of up to 50% of eligible expenditure
+ Ceiling: 50 Lakhs per application
- Disbursed in tranches based on the following milestones:
i. Completion of design and simulation: 10%
ii. Prototype development and validation: 10%
iii. Demonstration in operational environment: 10%
iv. Readiness for volume production or deployment: 20%
