12.2.2 Capital subsidy shall be paid in 7 equal instalments where the first 
instalment will become due after the concerned Medical Establishment 
obtain entry level NABH accreditation. The second instalment will become 
due after the concerned Medical Establishment obtain final level NABH 
accreditation. The remaining 5 instalments shall get due on each 
subsequent anniversary of the date on which final NABH accreditation was 
obtained, subject to the Medical Establishment maintaining the NABH 
accreditation throughout the incentive period and thereafter. 
12.2.3 For computing the limits set forth herein (for Capital Subsidy) Capital 
Investment / TPC would be the lowest of:
• 
Total Capital Investment / TPC mentioned in DPR while submitting the 
application; or
• 
Total Capital Investment / TPC as agreed between lending bank and 
the applicant; or
• 
Actual Capital Investment / TPC as per certificate from Statutory 
Auditor of the Eligible Applicant.
12.2.4 In case of any projects with TPC above Rs 500 Crores in Category A districts 
and above Rs 75 Crores in Category B & C districts: no separate incentives 
shall be paid under this policy, except for the incentives approved by CCIP, 
either under any special package or under this Policy.
