3. DEFINITIONS 
3.1. DATE OF COMMENCEMENT OF COMMERCIAL PRODUCTION 
The date of issue of the first sale invoice, filed as the ‘Date of 
Commencement of Commercial Production’ under IEM (Industrial 
Entrepreneur Memorandum) Part-B, by a new unit or after expansion, 
diversification, or technological upgradation.  
For the purpose of incentives, the investment or turnover of the unit, as 
defined in the ‘Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises Development Act, 
2006’ (hereinafter referred to as MSMED Act, 2006), on the ‘Date of 
Commencement of Commercial Production’ shall be considered for the 
classification of the unit as MSME or Large Industry. 
3.2. DEPARTMENT 
“Department” means Department of Industrial Policy and Investment 
Promotion, Government of Madhya Pradesh. 
3.3. ELIGIBLE FIXED CAPITAL INVESTMENT (EFCI)

and dwelling units and GST paid on construction materials.
