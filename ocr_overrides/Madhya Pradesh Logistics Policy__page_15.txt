Pavarkheda, Itarsi Kesar Multimodals Logistics

The state has focused on initiatives to develop industrial parks, food parks, integrated cold storage
facilities, command and control centers, integrated border check points and Multi-Modal Logistics Parks

Industrial Development Corporation Limited (MPIDC) is driving industrial growth through strategic
initiatives. The state's focus on enhancing logistics infrastructure is aimed at positioning Madhya Pradesh
as a national logistics powerhouse.
