14. Grievance Redressal Mechanism:

14.1. There is a provision for CGD entities to redress all the complaints/issues raised
by general public/authorities/other public utilities within 07 days.

14.2. The complaint lodged shall be forwarded to the one concerned CGD entity and
the CGD entity shall address the same and submit the compliance against each
complaint within seven (07) days to the District Nodal Officer.
