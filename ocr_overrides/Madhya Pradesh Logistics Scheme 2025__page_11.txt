The sanction shall be done on milestone basis as below: 
 
First Instalment: First installment of 40% of the assistance shall be released

Second Instalment: 30% of the assistance shall be sanction on completion

6.1.2  Stamp Duty and Registration Fees 
 
1. The investor shall be reimbursed 100% of the stamp duty and 
registration fee. 
2. The maximum limit of the assistance shall be ₹ 5 Crores. 
3. The subsidy is applicable to units that has taken land on lease in the 
industrial areas established by the State Government or on purchase of 
private land. 
 
6.1.3 Assistance for Certified Green Logistics Parks and MMLP 
 
1. The investor shall be reimbursed 50% of the certification fee for Indian 
Green Building Council (IGBC) for Gold and Platinum certification. 
2. The maximum limit of the assistance shall be ₹ 20 lakhs. 
3. Reimbursement shall be done after obtaining the certification from 
Indian Green Building Council (IGBC)
