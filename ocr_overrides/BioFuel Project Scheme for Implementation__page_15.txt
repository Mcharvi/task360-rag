14.	 Procedure for Registration 
14.1.	
The Unit shall be registered by Commissioner, NRE Department, GoMP. 
14.2.	
The Biofuels developer will submit an application to Commissioner, 
NRE Department GOMP along with the following documents: 
a)	 Application in the prescribed format.
b)	 a certified copy of the Memorandum and Articles of Association of 
the company/bylaws of the registered society (as applicable).
c)	 Certified copy of the partnership deed (if applicable).
d)	 Copy of the accounts (balance-sheet) of the last three years; in 
case of startup, the balance sheet of the promoter company.
