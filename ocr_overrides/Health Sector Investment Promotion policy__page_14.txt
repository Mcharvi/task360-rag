Definitions
07
AERB
AERB refer to Atomic Energy Regulatory Board of India
Capital 
Investment/ TPC
Means the capital expenditure associated with setting-up the 
Medical Establishment, and shall include the investment in 
buildings, fixtures, medical equipment, furniture, plant & 
machinery, IT infrastructure but shall exclude the investment in 
land, working capital and operating expenses. 
Date of 
Commencement 
of Commercial 
Operation
Means the date on which certificate of registration under Madhya 
Pradesh Upcharyagriha Tatha Rajopchar Sambandhi Sthapanaye 
(Ragistrikaran Tatha Anugyapan) Adhiniyam, 1973 is issued to 
the Medical Establishment or the date on which the Hospital / 
Medical College commences its services in compliance with laws 
governing registration and operations of Medical Establishment, 
whichever is later.
Detailed Project 
Report
Means the detailed project proposal submitted along with the 
application seeking benefits or incentives under this Policy.
Effective Date
Means 24th day of February 2025, the date on which this Policy 
shall come into effect.
Investment 
Promotion 
Committee
Means the committee chaired by the Commissioner, Public 
Health and Medical Education and comprising of an officer 
nominated from finance department and various other officers to 
be nominated by the Principal Secretary, Public Health and 
Medical Education for applicants seeking incentives in respect of 
Multi-Speciality Hospitals, Medical College and Super Speciality 
(multi-single) Hospitals.
Government
Means the Government of the state of Madhya Pradesh.
Hospital
Means any premises providing medical and surgical treatment 
and nursing care for sick or injured persons, including one or 
more of outpatients, inpatients and diagnostics and/or day care 
services.
Medical 
Establishment
Medical Establishment refers to Multi-Speciality Hospitals, 
Medical College and Super Speciality (multi-single) Hospitals.
