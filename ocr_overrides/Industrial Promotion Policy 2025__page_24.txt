7.7 Technological Upgradation
Technological upgradation refers to units upgrading their technology (only related to production) and investing
at least 30% of existing investment (with a minimum of %20 Crore) or Rs.100 Crore in Plant & Machinery,
whichever is lower. The unit should be in commercial production for last 7 years for claiming technological
upgradation incentive (without the change of product). Any type of unit undergoing technological upgradation
