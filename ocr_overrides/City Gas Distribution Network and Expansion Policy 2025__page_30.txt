13.2.1. Periodicity of meetings:
The District Level Monitoring Committee (DLMC) shall be convened once in a
month or more frequently whenever needed, during the implementation of the
project. The status update on the review meeting shall be submitted to the
Directorate of Food & Civil Supplies, Madhya Pradesh.
