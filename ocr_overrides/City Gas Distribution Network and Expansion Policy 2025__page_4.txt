Chief Minister
Message

India is the fastest growing economy in the world. As envisioned by the Hon.
Prime Minister, India will be a developed Nation, For this the concept of developed
Madhya Pradesh has to be given a concrete shape. It is a matter of great joy that
the Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Protection Department is implementing
the City Gas Distribution (CGD) Policy 2025 to bring revolutionary changes in the
field of domestic gas distribution in Madhya Pradesh. Under this policy, the
distribution network of Piped Natural Gas (PNG) and Compressed Natural Gas
(CNG) will be expanded in the state, which will make clean and cheap fuel available
to every household.The aim of the state government is to attract investors, establish Madhya
Pradesh as India’s leading green energy hub and provide the benefits of clean fuel
to the people.

| am confident that the City Gas Distribution (CGD) Policy 2025 will not only
benefit the people of the state, but will also lead to industrial development and
construction of infrastructure on a large scale. On this occasion, | express my
heartfelt wishes to the people of the state.

(Dr. Mohan Yadav)
