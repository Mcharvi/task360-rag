The State has formulated 18 new policies after thorough collaborative consultation with the 
stakeholders. While these policies provide ﬁnancial incentives at par with the best provided by any 
other State, yet the focus is to provide seamless investment climate, exemplary Ease of Doing Business 
and reduction of compliance burden. State has already put in place mechanisms to streamline 
approvals, with faceless interface and time-bound clearances. Madhya Pradesh initiated the concept 
of the Public Service Delivery Guarantee Act and is committed to ensure that all approvals are notiﬁed 
under this Act. Providing plug and play infrastructure for industries is another important corner stone 
of the policies.
Madhya Pradesh Startup Policy, 2025 aims to build upon the strong foundations laid by the earlier 
policy. Startup ecosystem in the State boasts over 5000 startups with an impressive 47% led by 
women entrepreneurs. The policy is a harmonious extension of the Startup India Initiative. The 
establishment of a Startup Advisory Council under the policy will further strengthen the ecosystem by 
providing expert guidance, fostering collaboration, and ensuring sustainable growth for 
entrepreneurs across the State. The policy's cornerstone is the establishment of a mega incubator and 
the provisioning of a growth fund, seed fund, collateral-free loan through banks, interest subvention 
on loan and technical mentoring partnership with institutes of national importance such as IIT, NIFT, 
IIM, IISER, ensuring that the State's entrepreneurial ﬂame burn bright to help build Viksit Madhya 
Pradesh and Viksit Bharat.
