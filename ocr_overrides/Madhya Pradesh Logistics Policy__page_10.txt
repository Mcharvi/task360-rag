2.1. Air Freight Station (AFS) / Air Cargo Complex (ACC)
As notified by the Ministry of Civil Aviation, AFS is an off-airport common user facility equipped with fixed
installations of minimum requirement and offering services for handling and temporary storage of import
and export cargo, etc. ACC is defined as a facility within the airport or off-airport augmenting the air cargo
movement capacity. ACC should provide facilities such as handling, storage, cargo clearance and other
allied facilities for smooth operations.
2.2. Container Freight Station (CFS)
CFS is a facility for handling and temporary storage for customs bonded or non-bonded cargo under
customs control and empty containers may offer allied facilities for smooth operations.
