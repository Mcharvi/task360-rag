8.2 Additional Financial Incentives to GCCs
8.2.1. CAPEX Subsidy
CAPEX Subsidy at the rate of 40% of ECI maximum up to Rs 15 crore to ‘Level I’ GCC and up
to Rs 30 crore to ‘Advanced’ GCC unit in 5 equal annual instalments post commencement of
commercial operations within eligible investment period.
The land cost shall be excluded in CAPEX investment in case eligible unit avails land rebate for
the proposed project. Capex subsidy would be applicable only in case of creation of fixed
assets in the form of buildings and machinery.
