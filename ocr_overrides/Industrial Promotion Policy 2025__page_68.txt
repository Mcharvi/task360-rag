Annexure III — Export Multiple

The Export Multiple shall vary from 1.0 to 1.3 for exports ranging from 25% to 75% of the total production.
ExportMultiple(EM)=IF(ExportPercentage<25%,1,IF(ExportPercentage<75%,1+0.3*(Export
Percentage-25%)/50%,1.3))
