condition Para 13.1.2
13.5.2.	 The bid should either be on Tolling Tariff Model or Com-

13.5.3.	 Tariff for the projects connected at CTU, both within and 
outside the state, shall be evaluated at STU level, con-
sidering all applicable ISTS charges and losses. Suitable 
formula shall be provided in the bidding document for 
normalization of the tariff.

in the bid process, which is mandated to participate as
