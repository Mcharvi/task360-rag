6.11. MANDI-FEE REIMBURSEMENT 
1. Mandi Fee reimbursement is available to Agri, Dairy and Food 
Processing sector as per chapter 9.1 of IPP, 2025. 
2. 100% Mandi fee will be reimbursed on procurement of agricultural 
produce for a period of 5 years or a maximum of 50% of investment 
in Plant & machinery (whichever is earlier or lower). 
3. The plant and machinery in point no. 2 shall be limited to 
investments in main processing machinery upto date of commercial 
production.  
4. This facility of reimbursement will be available only to those units 
which purchase agricultural produce of the state. 
5. The unit shall provide the calculation of the mandi fees paid on the 
procurement of the agricultural produce of the State, verified by MP
