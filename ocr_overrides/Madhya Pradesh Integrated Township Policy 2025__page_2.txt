MESSAGE 
Urbanization is a defining force of economic and social progress. Madhya Pradesh is 
witnessing rapid urban growth, and it is our responsibility to ensure that this growth is well-
planned, sustainable, and inclusive. The Integrated Township Policy, 2025 has been crafted 
with this vision in mind to create modern, self-sufficient, and well-connected townships that 
enhance the quality of life for all. 
This policy provides a structured framework for private sector participation in urban 
development while maintaining strong governance and regulatory oversight. By enabling 
planned infrastructure, streamlined approvals, and incentives for affordable housing, we are 
creating an ecosystem that fosters investment, innovation, and economic prosperity. 
A key highlight of this policy is its emphasis on sustainability integrating green spaces, 
promoting eco-friendly infrastructure, and ensuring efficient resource utilization. Our goal is to 
build communities that are not only aesthetically appealing but also environmentally 
responsible and economically viable. 
I acknowledge that this document is the result of extensive consultations with 
experienced developers, think tank institutes and government officials. I am confident that it 
will successfully achieve the objectives set forth in the policy.
