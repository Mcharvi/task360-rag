The Madhya Pradesh Startup Policy and Implementation Scheme 2025 has been formulated through 
extensive analysis, benchmarking against leading states, and adopting global best practices.  The 
policy takes a startup-centric approach aligning with ecosystem needs. Initiated in 2024, the 
Department of Micro Small and Medium Enterprises hosted Regional Industrial Conclaves across 
Madhya Pradesh and conducted investor, startup founders and other relevant startup ecosystem 
players interactions at national and international levels. Startup Ecosystem stakeholders were 
consulted to identify key challenges, which the policy aims to address.
The policy aims to enhance the entire startup ecosystem of the state and seeks to raise the number of 
DPIIT-recognized startups in the state to 10,000.
Madhya Pradesh Startup Policy and Implementation Scheme 2025 is a visionary initiative that provides 
comprehensive assistance to startups from the ideation to the implementation stage. One of the 
policy's standout features is the 'Entrepreneurs in Residence' support, which oϩers a safety net to 
entrepreneurs, cushioning them against capital risk during the critical ideation phase. It incentivizes 
startups with seed fund grant, startup capital fund of Rs. 100 Crore, collateral-free loans through banks, 
interest subvention under and operational assistance such as product development grant, patent 
assistance, lease rental support and marketing support. The policy also has special package for 
manufacturing-based startups.
