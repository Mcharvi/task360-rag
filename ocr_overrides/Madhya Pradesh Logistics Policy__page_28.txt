Incentives for creating Logistics 12
Incentives under this policy will be available for entrepreneurs setting up Logistics and Warehousing
Infrastructure on government land as well as private owned land. The provisions for allotment of
government land within industrial parks or any other government land for setting up Logistics and
Warehousing Infrastructure will be as per the prevalent MP Land Management Rule for industries.
