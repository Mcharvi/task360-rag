Short Title
02
This policy is issued in supersession of the Madhya Pradesh Health Sector 
Investment Promotion Policy, 2019, referred to as the “Old Policy”, and shall be 
titled the “Madhya Pradesh Health Sector Investment Promotion Policy, 2025”, 
referred to hereafter as the “Policy”.
Vision
03
The Government of Madhya Pradesh, in partnership with private sector, is 
committed to provide unparalleled access to affordable, world-class medical 
facilities to all the citizens of Madhya Pradesh. It envisions a future where quality 
healthcare is not a distant dream but an immediate reality for every citizen. With a 
strategic focus on transforming the healthcare landscape, it aims to dramatically 
diminish the burdens of time, effort, and financial strain that healthcare can impose 
on the people of the state.
