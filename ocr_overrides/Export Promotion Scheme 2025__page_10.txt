6.5. EXPORT TURNOVER INCENTIVE AND EXPORT GROWTH ACCELERATOR 
ASSISTANCE  
1. The incentive shall be applicable on incremental export from the second 
year of export operations (i.e. 12 months after date of first export 
consignment) and shall be provided for a maximum period of five years. 
2. The claim shall be submitted on the annual basis not later than 180 
days after the end of every financial year during the eligibility period. 
MD, MPIDC may condone the delay. 
3. For availing these incentives, the unit shall furnish CA certificate 
verifying the annual turnover, annual incremental FOB value of 
Exports, etc. (Ref Form - II) 
i. Export Turnover Assistance 
a. To reward sustained export growth, eligible units shall receive an 
incentive equivalent to 10% of the annual incremental Free on Board 
(FOB) value of exports. 
b. The total assistance available under this sub-scheme shall not 
exceed ₹2 crore per unit for the entire scheme / policy period. 
c. This assistance shall be maximum for 5 years.
