Delegation of Power

e Any power or duty conferred or imposed by this policy on the State Government may
be exercised or discharged by the State Government to the Civil Aviation Department
or any other Office specially empowered in this behalf by the State Government. The
State Government may from time to time, by order issue directions consistent with the
provision of this policy or by any person authorized by it.e Any power or duty conferred or imposed by these rules on the Aviation Department
may be exercised or discharged by the Aviation Department or by any other person
authorized by the Aviation Department on its behalf.
