15.	 Miscellaneous Provisions: 
15.1.	
It will be mandatory for biofuel projects to be established under this 
policy to comply with the relevant environmental laws, regulations 
and orders. 
15.2.	
A high-powered committee will be constituted under the 
chairpersonship of Chief Secretary of Madhya Pradesh for 
monitoring, reviewing, interdepartmental coordination, redressal 
of difficulties and better implementation of the Policy.
15.3.	
Under this Policy, facilities and incentives will be payable only on 
technologies approved by Ministry of New and Renewable Energy 
or under the National Biofuels Policy 2018 (as amended). Mixing 
of fossil-based fuels with waste/biomass will not be permitted.
