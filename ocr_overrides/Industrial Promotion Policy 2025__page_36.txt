Garment & Apparel, Footwear, 
Toys, and Accessories Policy
These sectors are crucial employment-intensive industries in Madhya 
Pradesh, playing a key role in providing job opportunities, especially 
for women. Building on its strong foundation in textile production, the 
State is increasingly focusing on expanding its garment and footwear 
manufacturing capabilities. For furthering this foundation special 
incentives such as Assistance for Employment Generation, Training 
& Skill Development, and Interest Subsidy have been introduced. To 
further incentivise this sector, the State has come up with Concession 
in Development Charge, Incentive for Apparel Training Institute, 
Reimbursement of Stamp Duty & Registration Fee and Power Tariff
