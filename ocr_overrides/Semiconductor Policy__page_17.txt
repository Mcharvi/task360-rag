4.4.2. Eligibility criteria for Semiconductor & Component Manufacturing
The following criteria will apply to units based on the approval status of their projects by the
Government of India (Gol).
4.4.2.1. Electronics and Semiconductor Projects approved by Gol
Semiconductor design or manufacturing project by any company/consortia/joint venture which
has been qualified under any of the following schemes of the Government of India shall be
eligible under this policy:
a) Semiconductor Fabs; any wafer size, any technology node
b) Display Fabs; TFT-LCD (Generation 8 and above); AMOLED (Generation 6 and above)
c) Compound Semiconductor Fabs; Silicon Photonics Fabs, ATMP/OSAT units
d) Scheme for Promotion of Manufacturing of Electronics Components and
Semiconductors (SPECS and SPECS 2.0)
e) Any other such scheme related to semiconductor or component manufacturing by the
Government of India
f) Refer Annexure A3 for list of goods eligible
